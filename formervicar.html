St.Mary's Jacobite Syrian Orthodox Church Muscat

OMAN

St. Mary's Jacobite Syrian Orthodox Church Muscat came into existence in the year 1980 A.D. As an integral part of the Universal Syrian Orthodox Church with the Patriarch of Antioch, His Holiness Moran Mor Ignatius Aphrem II as its supreme head., it is a remnant of the most ancient Christian church that was born in Jerusalem and brought up in Antioch.
